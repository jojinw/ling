About Me

I am a PhD candidate at the Department of Linguistics and Cognitive Science, University of Delaware. My research interests span across syntax, semantics, and psycholinguistics. The narrower interests include syntactic analyses of word-formation, the syntax and semantics of argument structures and valence-changing operations, among others. I am advised by Professor Benjamin Bruening.
